📖 Key Concepts

Independence of Motion — Horizontal and vertical components are independent. Gravity only acts vertically; horizontal velocity stays constant (no air resistance).

Optimal Angle — Maximum range is always achieved at 45°. Try it! Equal angles above and below 45° give the same range (e.g. 30° = 60°).
